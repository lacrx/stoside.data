The annual operating budget document  
serves as the City’s monetary statement 
of program and service delivery for the 
next twelve months.  This brochure has 
been developed to show a snapshot of 
the general fund budget. 
 
The COVID-19 pandemic and resulting 
economic changes impacted all major 
revenue categories.  As a result, the 
revenue forecasted in January has been 
reduced by $8.89M with the biggest 
impact on Property Tax, Sales Tax and 
Transient Occupancy Tax. 
 
In order to balance the budget, the  
expenditures were reduced $7.98M, 
mostly from anticipated salary savings 
from vacant positions, Maintenance & 
Operations and Internal Service Funds.   
 
Given the current economic uncertainty, 
staff will be closely monitoring the 
revenues and will be following the 
budget restrictions instituted by the City 
Manager to control spending.  The 
package of cost containment efforts 
includes: 
 
 Freeze on recruitment and 
replacement hiring 
 Freeze on employee travel for 
conference/training 
 Reduction of maintenance and 
operation budgets by 10 percent 
 Contracting services where possible    
 
The FY 2020-21 General Fund budget 
will not use any one-time money to 
cover expenditures but will be funded 
through actual revenues. The adopted 
budget 
is 
fiscally 
conservative, 
maintains service levels, and continues 
to focus on the  Council’s collective 
priorities.
