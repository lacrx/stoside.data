EXECUTIVE SUMMARY 
 
The annual operating budget document serves 
as the City’s monetary statement of program and 
service delivery for the next 12 months.  This 
brochure has been developed to show a 
snapshot of the general fund budget. 
This budget represents the collaborative work 
between the City Manager’s Office and all City 
Departments.  It represents staff’s best efforts, 
while relying on industry experts and economic 
forecasts to anticipate the City’s economic future 
and budget accordingly. The latest economic 
forecasts developed by industry experts reflect 
considerable uncertainty due to the current 
economic environment.  The United States is 
currently experiencing the highest pace inflation 
in 40 years with a CPI of 9.1%.  The current 
economic environment was considered when 
developing the FY 2022-23 budget. 
The City’s General Fund supports the majority of 
City services and comprises approximately 37 
percent of the total operating appropriations. 
The revenue projections were conservatively 
estimated based on the best available 
information.  The property and sales taxes were 
budgeted using the forecasts provided by an 
industry expert, HdL Companies.  
To address inflation, staff added a 5% cost of 
living to the Maintenance and Operations 
expenses.   Also included in the budget were 
known price increases to contracts above 5%, 
expected increases to fuel costs, known 
bargaining agreement costs and reasonable 
placeholders for future increases to respond to 
the competitive labor market and wage growth 
seen nationwide.  With talk of a possible 
recession on the horizon, staff will continuously 
monitor the status of the City’s revenues and 
expenses.  If needed, adjustments will be made 
to cut back on expenses to manage City costs.
